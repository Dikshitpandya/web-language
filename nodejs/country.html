States of India

  • Andhra Pradesh

    Andhra Pradesh is a state in the southeastern part of India. It is known for its rich cultural heritage and historical landmarks.

  • Arunachal Pradesh

    Arunachal Pradesh is a northeastern state with breathtaking landscapes and a diverse range of tribes and cultures.

  • Assam

    Assam, in the northeastern region, is known for its tea plantations, wildlife, and unique culture.

  • Gujarat

    Gujarat is a western state known for its vibrant culture, historical sites, and delicious cuisine, including famous dishes like Dhokla and Thepla.

  • Maharashtra

    Maharashtra is a western state famous for the bustling city of Mumbai, historical landmarks, and its contribution to Indian cinema.

  • Rajasthan

    Rajasthan, in the northwest, is renowned for its royal palaces, forts, and a rich tapestry of art and culture.
